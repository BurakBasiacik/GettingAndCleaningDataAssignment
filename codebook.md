# TidyData.txt CodeBook

Subject: ID of the subject
Activity: Type of activity done by the subject. There are six types of activities recorded:

- LAYING
- SITTING
- STANDING
- WALKING
- WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
- WALKING_UPSTAIRS
